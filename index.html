What Open Source Is?

Open source refers to software whose source code is publicly available for anyone to inspect, modify, and distribute.

✨

Benefits of Open Source

Free from vendor lock-in and proprietary restrictions. Free to modify and distribute.

✨

Negatives of Closed Source

Closed source means vendor lock-in, limited customization, and dependency on the original developer.
